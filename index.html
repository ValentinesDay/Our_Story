    • first datejune 15
    There once was a geeky boy & a slightly nerdy girl each searching for each other, but never knowing it. One day the meet online & exchange message. This leads to the geeky boy asking the slightly nerdy girl out. The geek & the slightly nerdy girl meet at Bad Apple & sit down for burgers, beers, conversation about everything! They realized their food was done, but neither wanted to leave the other, so across the street to a bar they went. They continued to drink & talk & talk & drink, until the slightly nerdy girl must leave. She makes a quick goodbye & hopes he'll text her again.

    brewery tour june 22
    The text comes and it’s an invitation to tour their local brewery. The following week, the slightly nerdy girl finds herself meeting the geeky boy in line (& a slight drizzle). One great tour & three drinks later, the two start a search for food. This will not be the last time they ask each other "Where do you want to go?" They end up at Fountainhead for a roof top lunch. Talk turns to friends, family, birthdays (9/22 for her & the following Friday for him!) & more. They talk &drink in the sunshine until he must meet a friend to watch the Blackhawks playoff game.

    hawks win!june 24
    The Blackhawks win & the championship is the following Tuesday. The geeky boy & slightly nerdy girl make plans to watch the game at a bar. They watch the game, cheer for each goal, eat, drink some beers & sit on the edge of their seats as the game changes from a certain loss to a win in the last minute of the game. Throughout the night & in the excitement of the championship win both feel the desire to kiss each other but the opportunity passes them by. The geeky boy escorts the slightly nerdy girl to a taxi --- next time they both say!

    birthday & first kissjune 28
    That following Friday (the geeky boy’s birthday) they find themselves at a restaurant ordering a bottle of wine & eating delicious food. Afterwards they move to a loud bar where their mutual poor hearing forces them to sit closer & close. Every inch closer and each touch is electric! After confirmation that it is no longer his birthday (first kisses shouldn't very be confused with birthday kisses) they exit the bar & kiss for the first time!

    summer romance turns into fall romancefall 2013
    The geeky boy & slightly nerdy girl continues the dinners, drinks, and activities - picnics, music in the parks, street festivals, cheering on half marathon (& bringing much needed iced coffees), flowers, the slightly nerdy girls birthday, Lincoln Park Zoo lights, nights watching Break Bad, lazy Sundays filled with football & cooking, meeting each other’s family & friends, spending Thanksgiving at the geeky boys family, one fantasy football champion, a hundred bets, a thousand laughs, a million text messages, & billion of kisses brings is to New Year's Eve.

    new year's eve 2014december 31
    After exchanging Christmas gifts (a wireless soil moisture sensor from him & a thesaurus & Settlers Of Catan from her) the couple take in a quiet dinner at Francesca's & then set off to Emporium for midnight toasts of Malört. Down the snowy street they walked when the slightly nerdy girl slightly blurted out "I love you!"

    valentine's dayfebruary 14
    A cold January starts off, but not for this couple! Dining out & cooking, building robots, meeting friends, going to parties, skiing on “mountains” & making future plans - Oscar Movie Marathons, additions & subtraction to the dinning list, cashing in on bets, lazy Sundays & celebrating holidays keep this couple warm & looking towards the future. So on this Valentine's Day, the slightly nerdy girl learns coding (well some) & builds a website for now & the future!
